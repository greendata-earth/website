Green Data Earth aims to analyse the output of agroecological and grazing activities in mountainous territory using data analysis and pattern recognition technology, in order to amplify the potential of these initiatives based on ancestral knowledge, regenerative movement and extensive grazing, providing tangible data to offset the negative effects of digital activity.
